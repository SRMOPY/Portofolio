01 // Who I Am

About Me

I'm a senior high school student from Indonesia who stumbled into cybersecurity the honest way — by breaking things and needing to know why they broke. My interest is almost entirely on the offensive side: understanding attack surfaces, chaining vulnerabilities, and thinking like the adversary.

I compete in CTFs when I can, build tools when something doesn't exist yet, and write things down so future-me doesn't have to relearn them. The goal is to get good enough at breaking systems that I can actually help defend them.

Currently looking toward university applications and early-career opportunities in offensive security. If you're building something interesting in this space — let's talk.
