01//Who I Am

Breaking things
to understand them.

I'm a senior high school student from Indonesia who stumbled into cybersecurity the honest way — by breaking things and needing to know why they broke. My interest is almost entirely on the offensive side: understanding attack surfaces, chaining vulnerabilities, thinking like the adversary.

I compete in CTFs, build tools when something doesn't exist yet, and write things down so future-me doesn't have to relearn them. The goal: get good enough at breaking systems to actually help defend them.

Currently looking toward university applications and early-career opportunities in offensive security. If you're building something interesting — let's talk.
